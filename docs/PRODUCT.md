# Nafas — Product Overview

> Honest snapshot. What the app is *meant* to be, and what actually works today.
> For the per-feature works/mocked/dead matrix see [FEATURES.md](FEATURES.md).

## One-liner

Nafas (Arabic **نَفَس**, "breath") is a **multi-country fitness social super-app
for the MENA region** — it tries to be Strava + MyFitnessPal + a coach/gym
marketplace + local discovery, in one Arabic-first app. Tagline in the app:
*"Your smart fitness companion / رفيقك الذكي للياقة البدنية"*.

## Target market & signals

- **Geography:** **Jordan is the primary launch country**, Saudi Arabia next, and the system must support **adding more countries** without a rebuild. A `Country` entity (currency, locale, timezone, phone code) is a Phase-0 foundation. *Caveat: today's prototype hardcodes Riyadh coordinates/addresses and `SAR` everywhere — that is exactly what the real build replaces (Jordan/Amman + JOD first).*
- **Currency:** per-country `Money {amount, currency}` — **JOD (Jordan) primary, SAR (KSA)**, extensible. The current mock's flat `SAR` is a prototype artifact to remove.
- **Language:** Arabic + English (i18n scaffolded, partial). RTL is implied by the market but not audited.
- **Audience:** gym-goers, calisthenics/CrossFit/running crowd, and the coaches/gyms/healthy-food businesses that want to reach them.

## Who the app serves (two personas)

1. **Athlete** — the default user. Tracks workouts & nutrition, follows people, posts to sport communities, finds training partners, discovers gyms/coaches/restaurants/tournaments.
2. **Coach** — selectable at registration (specialty, years, certifications collected). Intended to list services, courses, before/after results, and take bookings. *Today the coach role is captured at signup but never used afterward — there is no coach-side dashboard.*

## Intended monetization (designed, not built)

The pricing model is visible in the mock data even though no payment exists:

- **Coach sessions** — per-session price in SAR (e.g. 120–200 SAR). "Book Session" button is dead.
- **Coach courses** — multi-week programs (e.g. *Handstand Mastery*, 500 SAR / 8 weeks). No purchase path.
- **Gym memberships** — Monthly/Quarterly/Yearly plans (150–3200 SAR). "Subscribe"/"Join" dead.
- **Restaurant orders** — macro-tracked meals with prices. "Order Now" dead.
- **Tournaments** — entry + SAR prize pools. "Register" dead.

So the commercial intent is a **marketplace take-rate / lead-gen** model layered on
a free fitness-tracking + social core. None of it transacts yet.

## The "AI" in AI Coach — what it really is

Marketing label. There are **no LLM/API calls anywhere**. Every "AI" output is
rule-based or canned:
- Workout `aiInsight` strings are generated by a local heuristic from completion %, volume, and rep PRs (`live-workout.tsx`).
- The "AI Weekly Plan" is a `generateAIPlan()` switch over 4 goal types returning fixed workouts.
- "AI Recommendations" and InBody "insights" are hardcoded suggestions keyed off the user's goal and simple deltas.
- `aiTips` is a static string array in `mock-data.ts`.

This is a genuine product-enhancement opportunity (real LLM insights), not an existing capability.

## Current maturity

**Design/UX:** advanced — polished, animated, themed, coherent.
**Functionality:** prototype — local-only, no backend, no payments, no real auth,
social state is ephemeral. Suitable for demos, investor walkthroughs, and as a
spec for the real build. See [ARCHITECTURE.md](ARCHITECTURE.md) for why, and
[FEATURES.md](FEATURES.md) for exactly what's live.
