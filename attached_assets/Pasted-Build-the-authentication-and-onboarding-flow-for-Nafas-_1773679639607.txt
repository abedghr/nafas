Build the authentication and onboarding flow for Nafas, a smart fitness companion app.

## Business Logic

### Registration
Two paths to join Nafas:

**Social Sign-Up (Google / Apple)**
User signs up instantly with no verification step needed — trust is inherited 
from the social provider. User lands directly on home screen and is nudged 
to complete their profile at their own pace.

**Standard Sign-Up (Email / Password)**
User signs up with basic info (name, email, password) and must verify their 
email via OTP before accessing the app. This ensures only real users enter 
the platform and keeps the user base clean and trustworthy.

Once verified, the user lands on the home screen. The app will detect that their 
profile is incomplete and prompt them to finish it at their own pace — no friction 
at signup, better completion rates overall.

### Profile Completion (post-login, step by step)
After login, incomplete profiles are nudged to fill:
- Physical info (height, weight, age, gender)
- Sports interests
- Fitness goals

Each step saves independently so the user never loses progress.

### Coach Identity
All users are athletes by default. Any user can identify as a coach during signup 
by checking "I'm a Coach" and filling in their specialty, certifications, and 
years of experience.

However, being a coach on Nafas is earned, not just declared. Verified coaches 
are the ones who appear at the top of search results and carry a trust badge. 
Verification is based on a combination of: certificates, number of active 
subscribers, average client rating, and transformation posts (before/after). 
This system rewards real results and protects athletes from unqualified coaches.

### Login
Returning users log in with email/password, Google, or Apple.
Forgot password is handled via email OTP reset.

## Goal
The flow should feel fast, low-friction, and trustworthy — get the user in quickly, 
earn their engagement over time, and build a verified community of coaches 
athletes can actually trust.
